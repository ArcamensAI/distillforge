distillForge

Brand Guidelines & Identity System

Product Essence

distillForge is a smart proxy that routes LLM requests to the most cost-effective models without sacrificing quality. It automates model distillation, training, and deployment. Our brand focuses on cost reduction, efficiency, and seamless integration.

Visual Metaphor

The design merges 'distill' (refining, filtering, optimizing flow) and 'forge' (structure, creation, technological strength). The logomark acts as a geometric press—taking large inputs and forging them into precise, highly efficient outputs.
