The whole idea

One picture

Your app talks to a single URL. The proxy keeps the hard requests on your teacher and quietly serves the easy ones from a distilled student.

How it works

Change one URL. We do the rest.

No agents to install, no model training to manage, no MLOps team to hire. DistillForge Cloud sits inline as a drop-in proxy.

01 — POINT

Add the proxy to your config

Swap your base_url for <task>.<tenant>.distillforge.cloud. Every request keeps flowing to your teacher model exactly as before — now observed and FinOps-priced.

02 — DISTILL

We forge a student model

From your real, redacted traffic we build datasets and train a lightweight, CPU-class student for each repetitive task — classification, extraction, routing, scoring.

03 — REROUTE

Easy requests get rerouted

Once a student beats your quality bar, the proxy quietly serves it for matching traffic and falls back to the teacher on anything hard. Your bill drops; your responses don't change.

The endpoint

One hostname, addressable by task & tenant

Multi-tenant by design. Each task gets its own routing policy, student model, dashboards and budget — isolated under your tenant.
